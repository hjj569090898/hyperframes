# Asset Strategy

Use product evidence first. Stock footage is support, not proof.

## Priority Order

1. Preferred product assets from the brief or asset inventory
2. Product screenshots, UI captures, or real workflow clips
3. Stock footage or atmospheric motion only when product assets are missing or when a beat needs extra energy

## Stock Footage Rules

- Use `params.pexelsQuery` only when a scene benefits from supporting atmosphere or there is no strong product visual.
- Do not let stock footage carry the solution beat when real product assets exist.
- Do not use stock footage as fake proof. Proof should come from product evidence, metrics, or trust markers.
- Good stock use cases: hook atmosphere, team context, office energy, transition coverage, broad category framing.

## Query Writing

Write short, visual queries:

- `modern SaaS dashboard`
- `team analytics workspace`
- `startup operations workflow`

Avoid long, slogan-like queries or literal page copy.

## Failure Signals

- Every scene uses stock even though screenshots exist
- Product UI shows up only once at the end
- Stock footage implies proof that the page never claims
- The chosen query is a sentence instead of a searchable visual phrase
